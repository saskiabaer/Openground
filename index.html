London Openground


Finding Green in the Grey. The pace of life in London can seem overwhelming and intimidating. Spending meaningful time outdoors can reap wonders for your physical and mental health - don't let the pressure of the capital stand between you and life's most simple pleasures. London Openground is your gateway to the hidden green spaces that offer sanctuary from the capital's chaos.
